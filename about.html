Avenged Sevenfold

ABOUT

Avenged Sevenfold is an American heavy metal band from Huntington Beach, California, formed in 1999.

The band's current lineup consists of lead vocalist M. Shadows, rhythm guitarist and backing vocalist Zacky Vengeance, lead guitarist and backing vocalist Synyster Gates, bassist and backing vocalist Johnny Christ, and drummer Brooks Wackerman.
